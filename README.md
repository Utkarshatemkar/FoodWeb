# FoodWeb
I’m MyOnlineMeal, bringing your favorite food straight to your doorstep! From tasty curries to healthy meals, party catering to daily subscriptions, I make every meal delicious and hassle-free. Fast, fresh, and always ready to satisfy your cravings!
Foodweb - Online Food Delivery Service

Foodweb is a web application that allows users to explore menus, place food orders, and get meals delivered to their doorstep. It also provides services like party/event catering, healthy meal subscriptions, and bulk ordering.

Features

Online Food Ordering: Browse restaurants, cuisines, and place orders easily.

Bulk Ordering: Order in large quantities for events or offices.

Fast & Safe Delivery: Track your order in real-time with contactless delivery.

Party & Event Catering: Custom menus and professional service for all occasions.

Healthy Meal Subscriptions: Balanced, nutrition-focused meal plans.

24/7 Customer Support: Chat, email, or phone support whenever needed.

Special Offers & Discounts: First-order discounts, referral bonuses, and weekly deals.

Technologies Used

Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Responsive Design: Works across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Animations & Interactivity: Smooth scrolling, fade-in effects, and hover effects
